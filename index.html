How to Install a Desktop Environment:

  1. For any "Not Installed" DE below, click the "Copy Command" button.
  2. **Open a Terminal** (usually `Ctrl + Alt + T`).
  3. **Paste the copied command** into the terminal.
  4. Press Enter. You will be prompted to enter your **user password**.
  5. Follow any on-screen prompts in the terminal (e.g., confirm installation with `Y`).
  6. Once the installation is complete, **restart your computer** or **log out**.
  7. At the login screen, look for a small icon (often a gear, settings cog, or session selector) usually near your username or at the bottom of the screen. Select your newly installed DE from there.
